About

Short version

I grew up in Colorado, moved to Sarasota at ten, and ended up at Auburn on a Division I swim team before transferring to FSU for Computer Science. Outside class I tinker with product ideas, run a Florida LLC, and read more about the history of how technology actually got built than I probably should. I also think technical writing and persuasive writing are a lot closer than most CS people admit.
