About

Short version

I grew up in Colorado, moved to Florida at ten, and ended up at Auburn on a Division I swim team before transferring to FSU to finish a Computer Science degree. My main interest right now is quantum computing — the hardware side especially — and I'm aiming for graduate work after the BS.

Outside of coursework I read about the history of invention, sketch out product ideas, and occasionally ship one. I think a lot about how technical writing and persuasive writing aren't as far apart as people pretend.
